# Perspective Camera Component

Perspective projection replicates human vision and is the most commonly used method for rendering 3D scenes.

### FOV

Specifies the vertical field of view of the camera frustum, measured in degrees from the bottom to the top of the view.

### Aspect

Defines the aspect ratio of the camera frustum.

### Near

Defines the near clipping plane of the camera frustum.
