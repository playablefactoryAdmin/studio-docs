In orthographic projection, the object's size in the image remains unchanged no matter how far it is from the camera.

<Image src="/studio-docs/components/3d/camera-orthographic-component.gif" alt="Orthographic Component" width={800} height={600} className="pt-6" />

### Left

Specifies the left edge of the camera’s viewing frustum.

### Right

Defines the right boundary of the camera’s frustum.

### Top

Defines the top boundary of the camera's viewing frustum.

### Bottom

Defines the bottom boundary of the camera’s viewing frustum.

### Near

Defines the near clipping plane of the camera frustum.

### Far

Sets the far clipping plane of the camera’s frustum.

### Debug

Enables or disables the camera debug lines.
