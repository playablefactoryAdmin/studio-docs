### Video

Video asset to show.

### Auto Play

Whether the video should start automatically when the object is added to the scene.

### Loop

Determines if the video should repeat continuously.

### Muted

Determines if the video should be muted or not.

### Use Unique Texture

Duplicates the texture that is used by the video and sets a new id to it. Unless you plan on changing the video, do not enable this.
