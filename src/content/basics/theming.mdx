# Theming in Studio

From the topbar, hover over the Theme setting.

<Image src="/studio-docs/basics/theming/theme-hover.png" alt="theme-settings" width={400} height={300} className="mt-6 rounded-xl overflow-hidden" />

You can select what theme you want to apply with this dropdown. If you would like to make your own theme, click on `Add New Theme` option.

<Image src="/studio-docs/basics/theming/add-theme-popup.png" alt="theme-settings" width={600} height={300} className="mt-6 rounded-xl overflow-hidden" />

Do not forget to add your theme name, and then click "Save Theme". Now you should be able to see your new theme when you hover over the `Theme` on the topbar.

To go back to the default theme, simply click `default`.
