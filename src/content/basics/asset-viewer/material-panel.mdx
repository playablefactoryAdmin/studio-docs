# Material Panel


**Material Panel** is where you can access and manage your materials. Materials are **not** available in the [Assets Panel](/basics/asset-viewer/assets-panel.mdx).

## Creating a Material

Click on the `+` icon on the top-left side and select the type of material you would like to create.

<Image src="/studio-docs/basics/assets-panel/assets-material-creation.gif" alt="creating a material" width={700} height={300} className="mt-6 rounded-xl overflow-hidden" />

## Changing the properties of a material

Click on one of your materials and you will see the properties of the material object on the Inspector panel.

For more information regarding materials, please check [Material System](/3d/advanced/material-system) page.
