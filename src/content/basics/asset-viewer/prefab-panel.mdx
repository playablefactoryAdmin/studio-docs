# Prefab Panel


import Image from "next/image";

In this panel, you can view your prefabs, add them to your scene, and change various properties of your prefabs.

You can also double-click the prefab you want to enter prefab edit mode.

<Image src="/studio-docs/basics/assetsview/prefabPanel.gif" alt="Prefab Panel" width={1186/2} height={693/2} className="pt-6" />

You can also include prefabs in your project from the arrow button in the top left.

<Image src="/studio-docs/basics/assetsview/importPrefab.png" alt="Prefab Panel" width={565} height={266} className="pt-6" />

For more details see [Advanced/Prefabs](/advanced/prefabs)
